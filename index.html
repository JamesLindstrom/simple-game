Simple Game

Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to pilot your ship through space.

Touching an enemy will end the game.

Your goal is to collect treasures to increase your score.

Collecting a treasure will also charge your ship's boost drive, allowing you to perform a single boost by pressing the spacebar.

Every fifteenth treasure will be a green hyper-treasure.

Collecting a hyper-treasure will supercharge your ship, causing it to create a powerful hyper-burst when boosting. This hyper-burst can destroy enemies, so use it carefully.

Press Any Key to Begin
