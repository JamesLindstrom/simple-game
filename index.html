Space Aggregator

Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to pilot your ship through space.

Touching an alien x-fighter will end the game.

Your goal is to collect warp particles to increase your score.

Collecting a warp particle will also charge your ship's boost drive, allowing you to perform a single boost by pressing the spacebar.

Every fifteenth particle will be a cosmic particle.

Collecting a cosmic particle will supercharge your ship, causing it to create a powerful cosmic-burst when boosting. This cosmic-burst can destroy enemies, so use it carefully.

Press Any Key to Begin
